Harnessing Duckweed for India’s River Health

Our Objective

To establish a comprehensive river restoration initiative in collaboration with government and NGO partners using duckweed's phytoremediation properties. This initiative will focus on cleaning up contaminated river sections, enhancing water quality, and fostering local biodiversity. The project will involve regular monitoring, research, and community outreach to ensure the effective implementation and long-term sustainability of the duckweed-based river cleaning efforts.

What We Do

  • Duckweed Cultivation and Deployment

    • Cultivation: We establish and maintain dedicated duckweed cultivation sites to ensure a consistent and sustainable supply of healthy duckweed plants for deployment. This includes selecting appropriate species, managing growth conditions, and conducting regular quality assessments.
    Deployment: Our team strategically deploys duckweed in polluted river sections. We focus on areas with high levels of nutrient pollution, heavy metals, and organic contaminants. By carefully monitoring and controlling the spread, we optimize the effectiveness of duckweed in absorbing pollutants.

  • Collaboration with Government and NGOs

    • Partnership Development: We work closely with government agencies and NGOs to align our efforts with existing policies and frameworks. Our collaborative approach ensures that our initiatives complement broader environmental goals and receive the necessary support for long-term success.
    Policy Advocacy: We advocate for policy changes that support sustainable river management practices. By engaging with policymakers and stakeholders, we strive to create a favorable regulatory environment for duckweed-based phytoremediation solutions.

  • Community Engagement and Education

    • Outreach Programs: We conduct community outreach initiatives to raise awareness about the importance of river health and the role of duckweed in ecosystem restoration. Our programs include workshops, seminars, and interactive sessions with local schools, businesses, and residents.
    Volunteer Involvement: We actively involve volunteers in our cleanup activities, providing hands-on opportunities for community members to contribute to river restoration efforts. Through partnerships with local organizations and NGOs, we create a network of engaged citizens committed to environmental stewardship.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to support AquaRevive?

Donations can be made through the donation portal available on the AquaRevive web platform.

What are the primary objectives of the AquaRevive project?

The objectives are to develop an interactive web platform, raise public awareness, foster community involvement, facilitate collaboration among stakeholders, and address severe water pollution and health risks.

What data sources does AquaRevive use for water quality monitoring?

Data sources include the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), Delhi Pollution Control Committee (DPCC), Centre for Science and Environment (CSE), and on-site water quality testing, among others.
